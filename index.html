Welcome To Compare-Players

A Progressive Web-App that allows you to easily compare 2 current NBA Players.

About The App

This app was made for my final project for the IT202 course at UIC. It uses 3 API's, The Offical NBA API, an NBA Player Image API, and a Reverese Geolocation API.

The app also asks for your permission to use your geolocation to make a guess on who your favorite NBA team is.

The API's I used for this project are found here: https://nba-players.herokuapp.com/ http://data.nba.net/10s/prod/v1/2020/players.json https://locationiq.com/docs

If you have any suggestions on how to improve the app, send me an e-mail with any improvements/bugs!
